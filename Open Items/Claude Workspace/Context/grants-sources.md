# Grants sources (tunable)

## Tier 1 — databases & major funders
- Grants.gov (federal) · Candid / Foundation Directory · Instrumentl
- Lilly Endowment (lillyendowment.org) — religion / theological education
- ATS & In Trust Center for Theological Schools — sector grants/resources
- UNCF and HBCU-serving funders (Turner is ITC-affiliated)

## Tier 2 — regional / denominational
- Community Foundation for Greater Atlanta · Georgia Humanities
- AME Church connectional funding · denominational education funds

## Notes
- Grant-search MCP connectors exist (foundation grants, open grants, federal grants) and could
  power this live later; web search is the default for now.
- Always flag eligibility — many funders restrict by 501(c)(3) type, accreditation, or geography.
