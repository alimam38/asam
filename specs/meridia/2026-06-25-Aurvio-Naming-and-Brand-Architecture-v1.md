## 1. The decision

**Aurvio** is the flagship name — the single brand for the operating system that all the programs run from. One name, held, that everything else sits beneath. This ends the naming sprawl (canon weakness G8): the prior generation of equal-weight names (Sentiarch, Aegis, Meridia, Integra, Aletheia, WayPoint, Hypomone) is resolved into **one flagship + layers beneath it.**

- **Wordmark:** Aurvio
- **Pronunciation:** AUR-vee-oh
- **Type:** coined (ownable, trademark-defensible, .com-available) — the Stripe/Brex register: a made word you give meaning to.

## 2. Meaning & essence

**Aurvio = aurora / dawn ("aur-") + view / sight ("-vio") → "the dawn that gives sight."**

This is the founder's own articulation, captured verbatim as the brand's north star:

> "It showed them who they already were and gave them the pathway, the route, the directions to take so that everyone else could see their truth."
>
> "It gave them sight — both insight, like the apple did for Eve, and external vision, like windows give to what's hidden behind walls."

The product **gives sight**: *insight* (the inner eye — who you truly are) and *vision* (the outer view — your true position made visible to others). Beneath that sits the **positioning** core — it shows you where you actually stand and the route from there (the Financial Positioning System). Apple opened the inner eye; Windows revealed what was hidden; Aurvio does both, and adds the way.

## 3. Why Aurvio — decision log

A full availability-first + collision clearance pass (2026-06-25) eliminated the alternatives:

- **Dictionary/known words — all encumbered in financial services and/or no open .com:** Aletheia (academic/AI saturation), Meridia (withdrawn weight-loss drug), Meridian (crowded — banks), Sextant (multiple capital/advisory/fund firms), Oriens (multiple "Oriens Capital" firms), Vantage (VantageScore), Datum (credit-data bureaus), Cardo (**Cardo AI = "OS for asset-based finance," Blackstone-backed** — same lane), Skopia, Plumbline (Dynamics finance ERP).
- **First coinages — .coms taken / real collisions:** Veraxis (existing marks + USPTO TTAB dispute + a crypto "VRXS" Ponzi — disqualifying for a finance brand), Orin (NVIDIA chip), Auren (global accounting network), Meridio, Senda, Lodevia, Orent.
- **Second coinage round — open .com but flawed:** Veridio (open .com, but collides with **Veridio AI Ltd** + the funded **Veridion** credit-data firm); Oravio (open .com, but **Oravio LLC** already holds the name).
- **Aurvio — clean:** no company, no registered trademark, no social footprint found in the knockout pass; **aurvio.com / .ai / .io all available.** Cleanest name on the board; ownability is the whole game.

> Knockout clearance is not a legal opinion. Formal clearance + filing by a trademark attorney (Class 36 financial, Class 42 software) is required before the name is locked.

## 4. Brand architecture — the hierarchy

**Aurvio** is the parent/OS brand. Everything below is a layer or an app *inside* Aurvio, not a co-equal name.

**Corporate (unchanged):**
- **Eden Intelligence Group (EIG)** — the company / IP holder. ("Eden" lineage stays at the corporate level.)
- **Meridia LLC (GA)** — operating company. *[Open decision: keep "Meridia" as the operating entity, or rename to an Aurvio entity. See §6.]*

**The OS — Aurvio — and its layers:**
- **Integra** — the orchestration spine (routing, permission cascade, corpus management).
- **AIA — Architected Intelligence Asset** — the owned, classified, compounding corpus (on the NAS). The memory.
- **Aletheia** — the steward / voice (the "truth" persona). *Retained as an internal layer name, not the flagship.*
- **Faculties:** FPS (Financial Positioning System), the Bilateral Reader, Signal Engine, Trust Index, Specialist Council.
- **Runtime:** evaluating **Hermes Agent** (Nous Research, MIT, self-hosted, persistent memory) as the operator skeleton beneath Aurvio.

**Products / apps (run on Aurvio):**
- **WayPoint** — the financial experience layer: Core, Edge, Crown, Renaissance.
- **Crown's Eye** — K-12 / education (Signature Theory).
- **Manus** — the always-on / wearable interface layer (Phase 2–3).

**The flagship operator (proves the OS):**
- **Hypomone** — the membership lending institution for the misbanked operator. Arms: Hypomone Capital, Hypomone Financial Network Partners (HFNP), The Hypomone Collaborative. Instruments: The Charter, The Solera Principle.
