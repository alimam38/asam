## What counts as signal
- Agentic tooling & frameworks with a real artifact (repo, release, working demo) — not thought pieces
- MCP ecosystem shifts: spec changes, major servers, infrastructure (gateways, registries, auth)
- LLM capability jumps: new models/features that change what's buildable (context, tool use, memory, scheduling)
- Things that touch my stack: Claude Cowork/Code, Populi/SIS, QuickBooks/Gusto, Postgres, Docker/NAS, embedded analytics
- Competitive/adjacent signal for Plumbline (dashboards / SIS overlays / higher-ed SaaS for small institutions)

## What to skip
- Hype with no artifact; funding-round news without a product change
- Model-launch horse-race coverage (unless it changes what I can build)
- Anything already covered by the daily skills scout (Claude skills/plugins/MCP *directories* are its beat;
  this scout covers the broader software/AI marketplace and capability shifts)

## Output stance
Ranked ruthlessly — 3–5 top picks with a "why it matters to Ali" line each, every item linked,
and exactly one "worth a session this week" recommendation. A scan for discussion & adoption, not a feed.
