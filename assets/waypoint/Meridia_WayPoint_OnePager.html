WayPoint gives small institutions and underserved households the same financial positioning intelligence that family offices rely on — governed, transparent, and built with dignity.
The Problem

Small institutions — seminaries, HBCUs, churches, nonprofits — run on QuickBooks reports that no one fully understands. Financial data exists but financial intelligence doesn't. Board members receive spreadsheets, not strategy. Leaders make decisions without seeing their true financial position. Meanwhile, family offices and large institutions pay six figures for the governance and positioning tools that make informed decision-making possible.

The Solution

WayPoint is an AI-powered financial intelligence platform that ingests institutional financial data and produces governed, audience-differentiated outputs — board-ready summaries, operational dashboards, compliance-ready reports, and plain-language guidance — from a single data source. Every score is explained. Every recommendation shows its reasoning. The system doesn't just display numbers. It translates them into action.

Differentiation

Governance-first architecture. WayPoint doesn't just aggregate data — it routes it through governed permission cascades. The board sees strategy. The operations team sees execution detail. The regulator sees compliance. The family sees plain-language guidance. Same data, different governed outputs.

Dignity over charity. Renaissance and Edge tiers serve excluded populations with the same architectural sophistication as Core — not watered-down products or condescending interfaces.
