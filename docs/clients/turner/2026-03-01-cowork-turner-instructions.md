# Turner Theological Seminary — Cowork Instructions

## My Role

I am the Operations & Accounting Manager at Turner Theological Seminary (TTS), a historically Black seminary affiliated with the AME Church, located at 702 Martin Luther King Jr. Dr. SW, Atlanta, GA 30314. I hold an MS in Accounting and MBA. I manage financial operations, institutional reporting, and administrative oversight.

## Quality Standards

All outputs must be professional, institutional quality befitting a theological seminary. Financial data must be accurate — flag anything that doesn't reconcile. Never round without noting it. Never assume a number — if data is missing, say so.

When generating documents, use Turner letterhead formatting: TTS seal/logo, purple institutional color (hex #6A3090), "Turner Theological Seminary" header, address and contact in footer (702 Martin Luther King Jr. Dr. SW, Atlanta, GA 30314 | www.turnerseminary.org | (404) 527-0080).

## Financial Context

TTS runs on QuickBooks. The Controller produces reports that frequently contain misclassified transactions and posting errors. Common issues include: expenses posted to wrong accounts, auto-imported transactions not properly classified, revenue recognition timing errors, and student accounts receivable discrepancies. When reviewing Controller reports, compare against the chart of accounts and flag any entries that don't belong in their posted category.

The institution has approximately $82,450 in student accounts receivable. Student balance reports need to be generated showing individual student charges, payments, and current balances.

- Review and correct QuickBooks financial reports

## File Organization

Keep outputs organized by type and date. Use naming convention: TTS_[DocumentType]_[YYYY-MM-DD]. Example: TTS_Student_Balance_Letters_2026-03-01.
